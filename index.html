What We Do

We are the leading bottle and can return center in the Hudson Valley. We take all NY 5¢ deposit empties and get your money back to you. With returning your bottles and cans, you are doing your part in protecting our environment. Come to a Bottle Depot near you for a fast, clean, and convienent bottle return experience.


To make your wait even shorter, we have introduced precounting. You are free to count the returns you bring in beforehand and our employees will only count one bag for verification. Please make sure that your bags are labelled with the number of empties on each bag.
